Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

Merlot Gamez Studio (“we”, “us”) builds casual mobile games, including TileFrenzy: Triple Match. This policy describes what information our games handle and how.

What we collect

The game itself does not ask for, collect, or transmit personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Your high score and game progress, kept in standard device storage so the game can remember your best score between sessions.

Advertising

The game displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or receive information from your device to provide and measure ads, including a device advertising identifier and standard technical data such as approximate location (from IP address), device type, and ad interactions. AdMob's handling of this information is governed by Google's privacy practices:

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can limit ad personalization at any time via your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

Children’s privacy

Our games are intended for a general audience and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our game, please contact us and we will work to remove it.

Data retention

Game progress is stored on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes that data.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no account or stored profile to access, correct, or delete on our side. For information held by our advertising partners, refer to their privacy policies linked above.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will post the updated version at this URL and revise the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email MerlotGamez@gmail.com or visit our Support page.
